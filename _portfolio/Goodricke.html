The University of York is made up of ten colleges and all students belong to one of those colleges. At the top of each college is an elected Junior Common Room Committee (JCRC) of students who are elected to oversee the day to day running of welfare, sports and events for the students.

In December 2013 I was lucky enough to be elected as Creative Media Officer for the college, in charge of producing multimedia content for events and for merchandise for the college.

The culmination of this was producing a GoodFest 2014 film about the festival produced in conjunction with Film & Television students.
