GoodFest is a free music festival annually in Goodricke College, University of York. 2014 was the 5th anniversary and to celebrate a highlight compilation has been put together to celebrate everything the festival stands for.

The festival was held on the 14th June 2014 and featured headliners Coasts and Sons & Lovers. Other acts included in the video are: Sugarking, Dandy & DeLions, York Hornets.
